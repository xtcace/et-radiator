Our Story

22 Years of Keeping Mt Druitt Moving

E.T. Radiator has been a fixture in Mt Druitt since 2003. We started as a cooling system and radiator specialist and have grown into a full-service mechanical workshop — but cooling systems remain our heartland.

Our workshop runs with three experienced mechanics who between them have decades of experience across all makes and models. From your daily commuter to a work ute — we've seen it all and fixed it all.

We're authorised rego inspectors, which means we can handle your pink slip and blue slip on the spot without sending you anywhere else.
