Our Story

22 Years Keeping
Mt Druitt Moving

E.T. Radiator has been a fixture in Mt Druitt since 2003. We started as a cooling system and radiator specialist and have grown into a full-service mechanical workshop — but cooling systems remain our heartland.

Three experienced mechanics on site every day, handling everything from a simple oil change to complex engine work. We're also authorised rego inspectors — pink slip and blue slip on the spot.
